ASR Law

This document covers the legal system of the Axos Socialist Republic and any other entities under the same administration.

Civil Offences

These laws cover smaller non-criminal offences, such as trespassing or stealing lower-level goods.

Trespassing

  • Anyone may enter another’s property, but they must leave if asked by the property owner.
  • Refusing to leave another’s property is prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • Killing or harming a trespasser over 10 damage points is prohibited and may result in greater punishment than the trespasser.
  • Entering a restricted union zone without permission from at least a commander is prohibited and will result in punishment.

Freedom of Speech

  • Insulting the Lord is only prohibited if made in serious sense, not if made as part of a joke or sketch.
  • Insulting the Admiral is always prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • Insulting a general is always prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • Insulting a commander is only prohibited if made in a serious sense.
  • Insulting a soldier is only prohibited if made in a serious sense.
  • Insulting a civilian or equal no matter rank is prohibited and may result in punishment.
  • Insulting a person of lower rank is strictly prohibited and may result in great punishment.
  • Using “Skibidi”, “Alpha”, “Beta”, “Sigma”, “Omega”, or other brain-rot words is prohibited.
  • Racism and sexism are strictly prohibited and will result in punishment.
  • Leaking classified information or media is strictly prohibited and will result in severe punishment.

Building

  • Building inappropriate structures resembling a Swastika or male reproductive organ is strictly prohibited. The hammer and sickle is an exception.
  • Flying the flag of an official enemy of the state is prohibited.
  • Buildings must not exceed 10 blocks without a permit from the Lord.
  • Extending or branching a road adjacent to your property cannot be classed or named without a permit.
  • Constructing a cellar or basement is prohibited if a man-made chamber or Redstone cable exists below.
  • Mining under your property is prohibited without a permit.
  • Extending or branching adjacent railways, canals, or ice tracks is prohibited unless isolated on your property.
  • Littering on public or private property is prohibited.

Transport

  • Check for carts before crossing railways; you hold full liability for accidents.
  • Blocking or damaging ice tracks is prohibited.
  • Sailing boats on R-class roads is prohibited but cannot be punished.

Redstone

  • Flying machines require a permit.
  • Wireless transmission requires a permit.
  • Noteblock or jukebox projects require a permit.
  • Explosive projects are strictly prohibited.
  • Lag machines are strictly prohibited.

Criminal Offences

These laws cover larger offences such as murder or terrorism.

Murder

  • Killing another player is prohibited without general rank and good reason.
  • Inspiring or ordering a murder results in equal punishment.
  • Indirect killing (bombs, pitfalls, etc.) is strictly prohibited.
  • Killing villagers is prohibited.

Terrorism

  • Placing TNT carts on railways is terrorism and always punished.
  • Jamming telegraph lines is prohibited.
  • Sending flying machines to crash into structures is prohibited.
  • Planting landmines without a permit is prohibited.
  • Burning buildings without a permit is prohibited.
  • Kidnapping is strictly prohibited.
  • Making, buying, selling, or owning explosives is prohibited.

Theft

  • Stealing items is prohibited.
  • Stealing money is strictly prohibited.
  • Stealing the Lord’s crown is strictly prohibited.

Fraud

  • Identity theft is prohibited.
  • Forging money, permits, documents, fossils, or ore veins is prohibited.
  • Pretending to have fought in the Rubicona War is prohibited.
  • Forging ancient structures or shipwrecks is prohibited.

Protection

  • Damaging protected areas, creatures, plants, or lava sources is prohibited.
  • Mining or building in protected areas is prohibited.

Treason

  • Protests are allowed unless violent.
  • Disobeying commanders is prohibited.
  • Disobeying generals, the Allegiant General, Admiral, or Lord is strictly prohibited.
  • Starting riots is prohibited.
  • Going rogue or attempting to split the ASR is strictly prohibited.
