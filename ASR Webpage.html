We are the Axos Socialist Republic, a Minecraft empire based on the server REDACTED. Founded on the 15th of June 2025 by Lordos Axos, or AxoFX. The capital of the ASR is Axford, a large well-known city at the center of all the ASR maps.

Ranking

The ASR has a unique ranking system based around various legions broken into further divisions, all ordered top-down from the Upper Command, the truly highest-ranking members of the ASR. Below is a diagram of the current full system, including special forces.

Prefecture

Within the ASR’s borders are smaller divisions known as Prefectures, the main being Axfordshire, obviously surrounding Axford. Each prefecture contains its own sub-capital and possibly more settlements. Provinces are like prefectures but are detached from the rest of the ASR.
